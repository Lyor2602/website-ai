Usage of A.I. in the army

As of today, artificial intelligence is not used on a large scale in the army. Though engineers are focussing almost all their attention on creating AI robots who will help fight wars in the future, the army is just largely using one bot, The drone. Drones are used to scout enemy territories without any danger to human life. Drones can also be used to take out terrorists or compounds. Drones are responsible for taking out some of the heads of the terrorist group IS. altough drones are not fully AI, they still have to be controlled from somewhere, we still count them as AI. The engineers and the world population are scared that if we make a fully AI drone that if somehow will go rouge and this would cause some mayor problems.

There is one major breakthrough in the army AI world, the "Big Dog", this robot is mostly used for carrying heavy stuff and this robot can climb any surface. The soldier has to wear a tracker so that the Big Dog can follow the soldier around. When there is a gun fight a soldier can find cover behind the Big Dog and if there is too much to carry for one soldier the soldier can put the things on the robot and carry on with his journey.

Fundings in technology and research is really helping the military in expanding their artificial intelligence drones. Altough artificial is not used on a large scale, it is still a critical part of modern warfare. The US Department of Defense has overall spent 7.4 billion dollars on artificial intelligence research, China has also spent around 10 billion dollars on AI research and they are hoping to become the leading country in AI research and AI in the army.
