Avoid Putting People Into Harm's Way

  The first drones did jobs labeled as "dull, dirty and dangerous".
  While that still may be the case currently, there are also ways to utilize UAVs that prevent putting people in harm's way, such as:

    
 
  • Utility Companies saving risk by the inspection of high tension electrical towers with UAVs.
    • 
 
  • Some National Park Service Units are currently utilizing drones for resource management and fire management missions.
    • 
 
  • The delivery of critical supplies like medicine or communications tools to remote areas.
    • 
 
  • Relay radio and wireless internet hotspots to restore service after a natural disaster.
    • 
 
  • The use of a thermal imaging camera to find a lost person in remote areas (search and rescue).
